It was back in 2017 when i realised that doing something you don't love will ruin the the whole life. so i started to deploy more and more time towards finding things that i enjoyed and building Websites and Designing them became one.

"Every man with two hands has the fighting chance."

At the same time i got employed as a Electrical Testing Engineer at a company. I had to do this to pay my rentals and bills. My family supported me at every stage of them all, And i can't tell you how much i love them.

So around last year i was a graduate student and wanted to get into this Tech Industry by any means. I did a google search and first result was from a Ad.(Yes i was caught). It was from Teamtreehouse, an online learning platform where they has expert teachers teach you how to code.

"Every master was once a beginner."

Learning to code from treehouse was easy as it was structured for the beginner. Once i finished their Frontend Developer Path, I joined Linkedin's Learning Platform which was Lynda.com. While doing this all music was with me, having a song playing as you code was a good fit.

You can listen to this song on YouTube I listen when i code.
